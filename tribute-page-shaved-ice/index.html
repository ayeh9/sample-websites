Taiwanese Shaved Ice

Mango Shaved Ice from the restaurant Class 302 in Los Angeles.

Taiwanese shaved ice is just one of the numerous styles of shaved ice that exists. What sets it apart is the production method, as it involves creating a flavored block of frozen milk which is then later shredded into layers onto a bowl.

Various flavors include:

  • Milk
  • Green Tea
  • Strawberry
  • Mango
  • Thai Tea
  • Taro
  • Chocolate

After the ice has been shaved, it is usually further enhanced by toppings, which can include:

  • Red Beans
  • Coffee Jelly
  • Grass Jelly
  • Mochi
  • Condensed Milk
  • Tapioca
  • Various fruits
